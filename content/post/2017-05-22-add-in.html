I just discovered a couple of things:

  1. an R Studio add-in called CRANsearcher that, when you run it, prompts you for search terms and searches the whole of CRAN for anything that matches those search terms. (Thanks to @juliasilge on Twitter for this.) To install:
devtools::install_github("RhoInc/CRANsearcher")
  1. This inspired me to see what else was on my AddIns menu in R Studio. I also found options for creating a new blog post and “serving the site” so I can preview it. Unfortunately it adds the extension .rmd instead of .Rmd. I had to manually change it. But it does put things in the right folders.

I should look there more often.
